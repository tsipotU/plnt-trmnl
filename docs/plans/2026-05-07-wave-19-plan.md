# Part 1 — #202 Watering interval/next-watering date desync

**Files:**
- Modify: `packages/api/client/src/pages/PlantDetail.tsx:645-651` (chip rendering)
- Modify: `packages/api/client/src/pages/PlantDetail.test.tsx` (regression test)
- Read-only: `packages/api/src/scheduling/bin-packer.ts` (confirm shift behavior)

**Hypothesis (from recon):** The chip at `PlantDetail.tsx:650-651` renders `${plant.current_interval}d` (raw configured interval). The "Watered" toast at line 386 reads `data.next_water_date` from the POST `/api/plants/:id/water` response, which has been **bin-packed** by `packages/api/src/scheduling/bin-packer.ts` — it shifts dates ±2 days to balance load across locations. So `current_interval=10` with bin-packing shift of −2 produces `next_water_date = today + 8`. **Both are correct**; they disagree because the chip describes the *interval* and the toast describes the *scheduled date*.

The fix is therefore **communication, not logic**: the chip must convey that the next watering may not be exactly `today + interval` due to bin-packing.
